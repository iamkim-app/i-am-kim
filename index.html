      Privacy Policy

      How we handle your account and data.

      What we collect

      We collect only what is needed to run the service: account profile, community posts/comments, and basic usage needed for features (e.g., free usage limits).

      Location

      Nearby search uses your device location only when you tap Use my location. Location is not stored as a history.

      Third-party links

      Quick actions may open third-party apps (maps, taxi, translation). Those services operate under their own policies.

      Account deletion

      You can request account deletion in Profile. This removes your profile, community content, related records, and uploaded community images.

      Location Usage

      Location access is used only to show nearby places such as restaurants, transportation and travel information.
      Location data is not stored or shared.
